### 3.2 Bernoulli Distribution

* Formula:

$$

$$

**Example (EEE):**

* Circuit breaker has probability $p = 0.1$ of tripping in a day.

### 3.3 Poisson Distribution

* Formula:

$$

* Q: What is probability of exactly 2 faults?

## ğŸ”¹ 6. Expectation & Variance

**Example (EEE):**

* Three load values: {100, 200, 300} kW with probabilities {0.2, 0.5, 0.3}.

* Expected Load:

$$

$$

* Variance:

$$

$$

ğŸ‘‰ Meaning: Average demand = 220 kW, but variance shows large fluctuations.

ğŸ“Š ML Use: Variance helps measure data spread â†’ important for feature scaling & error estimation.
